# Lead criteria

Adjust what counts as "interesting". The agent reads this at runtime.

**Company types:** Startups, enterprise, agencies  
**Roles:** Founders, CTOs, engineers, PM  
**Industries:** AI/ML, dev tools, SaaS, fintech, healthtech  

**Green flags:** Company email, first agent deploy, technical + startup  
**Red flags:** Personal email for B2B, enterprise without clear contact
